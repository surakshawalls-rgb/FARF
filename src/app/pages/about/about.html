About Us

Fundamental Action and Research Foundation (FARF) is a Bengaluru-based non-profit organization dedicated to rural development, education, and research. We aim to bridge the gap between urban and rural India through impactful initiatives.

Our Purpose

Connecting eligible beneficiaries with Government schemes and providing essential digital services at their doorstep.

Scope of Work

💻

Digital Services

Providing essential digital services like income certificates, caste certificates, and more.

🎓

Child Education Support

Sponsoring education for underprivileged children in rural areas.

🏡

Adarsh Gram Yojana

Adopting villages to transform them into ideal rural communities.

📊

Rural Research

Conducting socio-economic and agricultural research to drive impactful policies.
