FARF has been working among under-resourced communities in Eastern Uttar Pradesh (primarily in Bhadohi and Jaunpur District) since 2015. Our vision is rural development in India, focusing on child education, skill development, financial inclusion, rural elderly welfare, digital services, government entitlements, and developmental research. Through the Common Service Center, FARF helps the weaker sections of rural areas access various government schemes.

Why does FARF need your support?

  • To provide education support, including annual school fees, dress, and books for children. We aim to arrange mini-school buses to minimize transport expenses for students.
  • To hire a full-time skilled person to expand our welfare services in villages.
  • To acquire tablets and hire teachers for digital classes in government schools, focusing on life skills and human values.
  • To recruit more volunteers for documentation, website updates, and social media management.
  • To invite experts and professionals to join our advisory board for collaboration and new ideas.

We invite you to partner with us in any of these needs. All your contributions via PayU are eligible for 80G deductions under the Indian Income Tax Act.
