Our Work Areas

FARF is committed to driving positive change through a diverse range of programs and initiatives. Our work spans multiple domains, each designed to address critical needs and empower communities for sustainable development.

Education & Skill Development

Access to quality education and vocational training for children, youth, and adults in underserved communities. Initiatives include scholarships, digital literacy, remedial classes, and career counseling.

Health & Wellbeing

Preventive care, nutrition, maternal and child health, and mental wellness. Health camps, awareness drives, and support for essential healthcare services.

Women’s Empowerment

Empowering women and girls through education, self-help groups, leadership training, and livelihood support, fostering gender equality and economic independence.

Livelihood & Rural Development

Enhancing agricultural productivity, promoting sustainable livelihoods, and supporting micro-enterprises for thriving communities.
