Our Work Areas

FARF is committed to driving positive change through a diverse range of programs and initiatives. Our work spans multiple domains, each designed to address critical needs and empower communities for sustainable development.

Education & Skill Development

We promote access to quality education and vocational training for children, youth, and adults in underserved communities. Our initiatives include scholarships, digital literacy, remedial classes, and career counseling.

Health & Wellbeing

Our health programs focus on preventive care, nutrition, maternal and child health, and mental wellness. We organize health camps, awareness drives, and support access to essential healthcare services.

Women’s Empowerment

We empower women and girls through education, self-help groups, leadership training, and livelihood support, fostering gender equality and economic independence.

Livelihood & Rural Development

Our rural development projects enhance agricultural productivity, promote sustainable livelihoods, and support micro-enterprises, enabling communities to thrive economically and socially.

Advocacy & Research

We conduct research, policy analysis, and advocacy to influence systemic change and promote social justice. Our evidence-based approach ensures our interventions are impactful and scalable.
