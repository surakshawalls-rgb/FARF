Completed Events

Rural Workshops 2024

On 24th September 2024, a rural workshop on “Digital Fraud & Financial Inclusion” was conducted by FARF in Bhadohi district (UP) with representatives from 60 villages and government officials. Additional workshops were held at Newada Kala and Sanathpur.

Plantation Drive

The plantation drive was conducted in collaboration with ‘OUR HAYATT’, with over 100 plants planted and community participation.

Motivation Program for Youth & School

Motivational programs for children, youth, teachers, and parents were organized in Gyanpur and BPMG School, Suriawan, with guest speakers and community leaders.

Charity Event

Two online charity events were held during the pandemic: a magic show by Illusionist Clifford and a concert by Bhojpuri singer Rajesh Pardeshi, raising funds for Cancer and Corona patients.
