About FARF

Fundamental Action and Research Foundation (FARF) is a Bengaluru-based non-profit organization working in rural development, agriculture, and research.

Since 2015, FARF has been empowering socially, educationally, and economically backward communities by improving access to government schemes, digital services, and awareness programs.

FARF has adopted multiple villages under the Adarsh Gram Yojana and actively works through training, community meetings, and research-driven initiatives.

Vision

To serve the basic needs and priorities of underserved communities through transparency, awareness, and access to essential services.

Core Work Areas

  • Digital Services: Income certificate, caste certificate, DigiPay, electricity bill, railway booking
  • Education Support: Sponsoring education for underprivileged children
  • Adarsh Gram Yojana: Village-level development and planning
  • Rural Research: Data-driven development strategies
  • Awareness Campaigns: Workshops and gram chaupals
  • Video Documentation: Highlighting real issues to authorities
