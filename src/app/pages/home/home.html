About FARF

Fundamental Action and Research Foundation (FARF) is a Bengaluru-based non-profit working in rural development, agriculture, and research. FARF empowers disadvantaged communities by connecting them to government schemes, digital services, and awareness programs.

Learn More →

Our Vision

To serve the basic needs and priorities of underserved communities through transparency, awareness, and access to essential services.
